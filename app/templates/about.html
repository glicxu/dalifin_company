About

We build agentic systems for complex decision environments.

Dalifin focuses on systems that do more than respond. They evaluate context, coordinate specialized roles, and execute within defined rules.

What we build

We design collaborative AI systems that combine planning, analysis, critique, and execution into one coordinated operating model.

The goal is not generic automation. The goal is structured intelligence that can be explained, constrained, and deployed in real workflows.

How the site is split

This repo owns branding, public copy, and product presentation. Release metadata and download decisions are read from app_server through a stable API contract.

That keeps the company site lightweight while the backend remains the source of truth for app publication and artifact resolution.

Operator-first, not demo-first.

We are interested in systems that can operate under structure, not just produce isolated outputs. That applies to markets, software delivery, research, and other domains where static software breaks down under complexity.
